Horoscope

A horoscope is an astrological chart or diagram representing the positions of the Sun, Moon, planets, astrological aspects and sensitive angles at the time of an event, such as the moment of a person's birth.In Hindu astrology, birth charts are called Kundali which are very significant and are based on movement of stars and moon. All the auspicious things are started after checking the kundali of a person including the marriage in which the birth charts of the boy and girl are matched.
